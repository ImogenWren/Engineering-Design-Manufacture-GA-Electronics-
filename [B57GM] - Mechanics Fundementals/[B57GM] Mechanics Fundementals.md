## Converting Units by Cross Multiplication

 $Q [Ua]/[Ub] = P[Uc]/[Ud] $
 
 Conversion Factors:
 
 Ua = gUc
 Ub = hUd

 Can be calculated:

$$

(Q * Ua)/(Q * Ub) *  (Q * gUc)/(Q * Ua) * (Q * Ub/Q* hUd) = (P Uc)/ (P Ud)


$$
